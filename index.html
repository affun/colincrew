Colin' s Story:
The full story.

On June 15, 2023, Colin Rogers was standing up for his friends who had been robbed the day before over a vape pen. The two individuals who stole from Colin's friends then tried to repeat it again, attempting to yank the pen from a friend of Colin. Colin grabbed the pen back while the driver of the car was holding it- then, the two thieves in the car grabbed onto Colin's arm and held it inside the drivers side window, dragging him 855 ft. down the road, before turning and letting go- causing Colin to fly out and land on the side of the road.

Instead of rendering aid, or even calling for help, the thieves sped away and left Colin for dead.

Luckily, one of Colin's friends who was there was able to call 911 in time, ultimately saving his life. Colin is still recovering from this brutal attack. Currently, he is a 7 out of a 23 on the coma scale.
